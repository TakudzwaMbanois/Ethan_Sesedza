About The Artist

Ethan Tanatsiwa Sesedza

The Unspoken E is a Zimbabwean Christian artist whose music blends heartfelt storytelling, acoustic worship, and soul-infused melodies. Known for his stripped-down, honest sound and impactful songwriting, Ethan draws inspiration from both local and global greats.

With a passion for developing original music, mentoring upcoming talent, and using music as a tool for community impact, Ethan is committed to building the Zimbabwean music scene with authenticity and excellence.
